City intelligence platform

Understand what's happening in the city — instantly.

Situate Vancouver is a real-time city awareness platform that brings together traffic, transit, weather, and public data into one clear, map-based experience — helping people quickly understand what is happening around them.

See how it works

A clearer view of Vancouver

Cities generate massive amounts of public information every day, but most of it is scattered, technical, or difficult to interpret quickly. Situate Vancouver brings that information into one place and turns it into something people can actually understand and use.

What it does

See what's happening, where it's happening, and what it means

Situate Vancouver helps people understand changing city conditions in real time. By combining transit updates, traffic patterns, weather conditions, and other public signals, the platform gives users a simple way to explore disruptions, delays, congestion, and ripple effects across Vancouver.

Built to make city data useful

Live map intelligence

Explore Vancouver through a real-time map that surfaces disruptions, congestion, weather impacts, and other important city conditions in one place.

Plain-language explanations

Ask a question or click an area on the map to get a clear explanation of what is happening and why, without needing to interpret raw data yourself.

Ripple effect awareness

Understand how one event can affect nearby roads, bridges, transit routes, and surrounding neighbourhoods — not just isolated incidents.

Open data, unified

Multiple public data sources brought into one streamlined experience, making city information easier to access, compare, and understand.

Accessible by design

Dark mode, light mode, strong contrast, and simplified summaries — designed to be clear and usable for a wide range of people.

Efficient and lightweight

Built for performance with a fast, efficient approach that supports accessibility, low digital overhead, and a smoother user experience.

Example queries

Built for real questions people actually ask

Why is traffic bad near Burrard Bridge?

Identify contributing factors such as collisions, construction, weather conditions, and corridor congestion, then see where the disruption is spreading.

What is happening near Waterfront Station?

See transit delays, crowd pressure, nearby disruptions, and area-wide movement patterns in one place.

Will rain affect downtown traffic tonight?

Explore how weather conditions may influence congestion, travel times, and route pressure later in the day.

Why is this area unusually busy?

View overlapping causes such as events, road closures, peak-hour traffic, service changes, or weather impacts.

Why it matters

Public data is only useful when people can understand it

Vancouver already has valuable public data, but most people do not have the time or technical knowledge to search across multiple websites, alerts, dashboards, and feeds. Situate Vancouver turns scattered information into clear, immediate insight that supports better decisions for everyday life.

How it works

Turning city data into clarity

Situate Vancouver gathers live data from public sources, organizes it into a consistent structure, and transforms it into map-based explanations that are easier to read and act on. Instead of forcing users to interpret fragmented information, the platform helps them quickly understand what is happening now and what may happen next.

One-liner (judges / intro)

Situate Vancouver is a lightweight, map-based platform that turns real-time city data into clear, human explanations of what is happening, where it is affecting people, and what will happen next.

Lightweight

Sustainable by design

Carbon aware

We keep the experience lightweight so maps and copy load quickly and use less energy per visit. Every page view is carbon-offset through Tree Nation, so exploring the city here does not add net emissions on your conscience.

Offsetting is provided by Tree Nation. Figures update as their widget loads.
